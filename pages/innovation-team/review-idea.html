Digital Currency Wallet

Submitted by Brian Ssempijja · IT Department · 10 Jul 2025

Description

A digital currency wallet solution that allows BOU staff and customers to securely store, send and receive digital currencies. The solution will integrate with existing banking infrastructure and provide a seamless user experience.

Problem Statement

Current payment systems are slow and expensive for cross-border transactions. There is no unified digital currency platform that serves both staff and customers efficiently.

Proposed Solution

Develop a multi-currency digital wallet with blockchain-based settlement, mobile app integration, and real-time currency conversion. The solution will leverage existing BOU infrastructure and comply with regulatory requirements.
