Benefits and Implementation

Describe the expected benefits of the innovation. Consider operational, financial, customer, regulatory, or strategic value.

Identify the resources required for successful implementation. Examples include: ICT support, funding or budget, skills or training, cross-functional collaboration, external partnerships, infrastructure or equipment.

Briefly outline how the innovation could be developed and implemented. Examples include: Proof of Concept (PoC), Prototype, Pilot implementation, Phased rollout, Full-scale deployment.

Describe how success will be measured. Examples include: reduction in processing time, cost savings, productivity improvements, customer or stakeholder satisfaction, revenue generated or costs avoided, risk reduction, compliance improvements, adoption or usage rates.
